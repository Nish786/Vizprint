About Us

What started as a passion and way of self-expression, became a business opportunity in 2015. Always an avid reader and an amateur poet, the inspiration to creative something with writing was an ever-present drive. As a freelance corporate video creator, with a creative set of designers, cinematographers and editors, the next step was to move into a business identity all its own. Thus was born Vizprint Media.



Our Niche

Vizprint Media aims at providing individualistic solutions to your corporate and social needs at a competitive price. An in-house team makes it efficient and cost-effective. Our aim is to provide complete print and digital support to the small and mid-size companies and be part of their growth curve. It gives us immense satisfaction to work with NGOs and help in their journey of providing care
